Product-Line Profitability Analyzer

Learning Goal

Evaluate profitability across product lines by comparing NMC, allocated overhead, and net profit. Identify when dropping a line appears attractive but lowers total company operating income.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app.

How to Use This App
  1. Select Financial or Marketing view emphasis.
  2. Drop or restore lines in the table to test contribution impact.
  3. Use the operating income panel to evaluate company-level effect.
  4. Use the worksheet to test a potential added line.
