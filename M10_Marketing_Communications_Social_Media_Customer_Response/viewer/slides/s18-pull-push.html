Pull vs. Push Communications

LO4 · Contrast
Pull Communications
  • Directed at end consumers
  • Objective: build awareness, preference, and loyalty
  • Mechanism: consumer demand pulls product through the channel
  • Tools: media advertising, digital/social, sales promotions, direct marketing
  • Approx. 2/3 of total comm. budget (industry norm)
Push Communications
  • Directed at channel intermediaries
  • Objective: build distribution coverage, shelf presence, channel effort
  • Mechanism: incentivize intermediaries to carry and promote the product
  • Tools: trade promotions, co-op advertising, channel financing, sales incentives
  • Approx. 1/3 of total comm. budget (industry norm)
Bottom takeaway
Pull creates consumer demand; push creates channel access. Both are required — but push without pull produces shelf space with no one asking for the product. Pull without push produces demand the channel cannot fulfill.
