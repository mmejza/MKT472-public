Message Reinforcement: Pulsing vs. Heavy-Up

LO3 · Contrast
Pulsing
  • Alternating on/off exposure periods (e.g., 4 weeks on, 4 weeks off)
  • Awareness builds → decays → rebuilds from higher base
  • Reduces copy wear-out through breaks in exposure
  • Lower cost than continuous — no spend in off-weeks
  • Best for: year-round brands maintaining baseline awareness
Heavy-Up
  • Intensive spend concentrated in the primary buying period
  • Awareness peaks just before and during peak demand
  • Minimal or zero spend outside the season
  • Maximizes ROI when purchase probability is highest
  • Best for: seasonal products — consumer electronics, snacks, holiday
Bottom takeaway
Pulsing controls cost and wear-out over a sustained period. Heavy-up concentrates GRPs at the moment purchase decisions are most likely. Neither is universally superior — match the strategy to the purchase cycle.
