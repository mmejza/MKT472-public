LO5 · Worked Example — Solution
StepFormulaResult
Current-period sales impact 12 × $20M $240M
Period 13 carryover 0.50 × $240M $120M
Period 14 carryover 0.50² × $240M $60M
Period 15 carryover 0.50³ × $240M $30M
Total multi-period impact $240M ÷ (1 − 0.50) $480M
Answer
Period 12 alone captures $240M ÷ $480M = 50% of the total impact. The other half is realized in future periods through carryover. A manager evaluating the $20M spend on current-period ROI alone is ignoring $240M in future sales.
Key takeaway
With c = 0.50, every dollar of current-period sales effect generates an equal dollar in future-period carryover. Cutting the ad budget based on short-run ROI alone systematically undervalues the investment by 2×.
