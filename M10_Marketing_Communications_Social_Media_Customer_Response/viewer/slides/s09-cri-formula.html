Customer Response Index (CRI)
A five-stage multiplicative diagnostic for communications performance
Formula
CRI = Exposure × Awareness × Comprehension × Intention × Purchase
StageQuestion it answersWho it applies to
ExposureDid the campaign reach them?Target market
AwarenessDid they notice the ad?Those exposed
ComprehensionDid they understand the message?Those aware
IntentionDid they intend to act?Those who comprehended
PurchaseDid they buy?Those intending
Two things to notice

Each stage multiplies the prior stage — a weak link anywhere collapses the entire index, regardless of how strong the other stages are

Diagnostic implication

CRI is a diagnostic tool, not just a measurement — the stage with the lowest rate identifies where budget should move first
