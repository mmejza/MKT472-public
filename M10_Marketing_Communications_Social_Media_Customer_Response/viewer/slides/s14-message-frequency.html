Message Frequency: Concentrated vs. Distributed

LO3 · Contrast
Concentrated
  • 13 messages in 13 consecutive weeks
  • Awareness peaks at week 13
  • No messages after week 13 → decays to near zero
  • Best for: seasonal products, political campaigns, events
Distributed
  • 13 messages spread across the full year (1 per 4 weeks)
  • Never reaches concentrated peak level
  • Sustained awareness — each exposure builds from a higher base
  • Best for: year-round brands, ongoing awareness maintenance
DimensionConcentratedDistributed
Peak awareness levelHigherLower
Long-run awareness maintenanceDecays to ~0Sustained
Copy wear-out riskHigh (compressed)Lower
Decision rule
Match strategy to purchase cycle. Seasonal/event = concentrated. Year-round brand = distributed or pulsed.
