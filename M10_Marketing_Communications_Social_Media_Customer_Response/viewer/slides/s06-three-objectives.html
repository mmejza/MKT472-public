Three Communications Objectives
Objective determines both creative approach and performance metric
Brand-Image Brand-Information Brand-Action
Brand-Image
  • Trigger emotional response
  • Build brand association
  • No explicit call to action
  • Nike, Lexus, FedEx
Brand-Information
  • Communicate differentiation
  • Shape consideration set
  • High recall of key attributes
  • Kyocera TCO Tracker
Brand-Action
  • Stimulate immediate response
  • Promote trial or purchase
  • Clear call to action required
  • Dell Deal of the Month
Key distinction

Objective determines both the creative approach and the performance metric. Misidentifying objective → wrong creative, wrong measure, wrong conclusion.
