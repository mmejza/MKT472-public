Causes of Low Customer Response

LO2 · Diagnostic

Each CRI deficiency maps to a specific communications failure. Fix the wrong thing and the CRI does not move.

CRI Deficiency Root Cause Correct Fix
Low Exposure Wrong media selected; coverage too narrow Broaden media mix; add channels reaching target
Low Awareness Insufficient message frequency; ineffective ad content Increase frequency; test creative formats
Low Comprehension Insufficient frequency; ad copy unclear or irrelevant Rewrite ad copy; align message with audience knowledge level
Low Intention Weak value proposition; frequency insufficient Strengthen offer; add promotional incentive
Low Purchase Ad copy does not specify action; competitor interference; service friction Clarify CTA; reduce purchase friction; address competitive gap
Diagnostic rule
Never apply a media budget fix to a copy problem, or a copy fix to a distribution problem. The CRI table identifies which layer is failing before money is allocated.
