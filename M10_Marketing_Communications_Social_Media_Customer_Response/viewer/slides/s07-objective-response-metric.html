Objective → Customer Response → Performance Metric

LO1 · Framework
Type Objective Customer Response Performance Metric
Brand-Image Build emotional association with brand Affective — feeling or emotion tied to brand Ad recall, brand recall, positive brand attitude
Brand-Information Communicate differentiation; enter consideration set Cognitive — interest in the information Key content recall, positive brand attitude, site visits
Brand-Action Motivate trial, purchase, or direct response Behavioral — urgency and call to action Purchase rate, click-through, conversion, response rate
Conclusion
Tracking awareness for a brand-action campaign, or conversion for a brand-image campaign, produces meaningless data. Match metric to objective before the campaign runs.
