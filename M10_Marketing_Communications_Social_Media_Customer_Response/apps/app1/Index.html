MKT 472 · Module 10 · App 1

CRI & Media Planner

Compare funnel conversion efficiency and media delivery intensity to evaluate why higher GRP may not produce higher CRI outcomes.

Learning Goal

Use CRI and media-mix metrics to compare campaign outcomes under different presets and manual input changes. Identify how stage bottlenecks can lower CRI even when GRP increases.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Baseline Campaign.

How to Use This App
  1. Select a preset or manually adjust CRI and media input controls.
  2. Use tab navigation to review both CRI Calculator and Media Mix outputs.
  3. Check the persistent comparison banner to track current CRI and GRP together.
  4. Review weakest stage and KPI outputs before entering answers in Canvas.
