Relative Market Share: Formula
RMS measures dominance relative to the top 3 combined — not relative to the leader alone
RMS = (Firm Market Share %) ÷ (Sum of Top 3 Competitors' Shares %) × 100
Components
Numerator — Firm's own market share %
Denominator — Sum of top 3 competitors' shares (not the leader's share alone)
RMSClassification
> 100Dominant leader — holds more share than top 3 combined
50 – 100Competitive — strong but not dominant
< 50Follower or niche — limited market leverage
Gate 2 Common Error

The denominator is the SUM of top 3 competitors' shares — not the share of the single largest competitor. Dividing by only the largest competitor overstates relative dominance and produces the wrong index value.
