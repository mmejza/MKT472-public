Industry Attractiveness: Six Porter Forces
Profit potential shaped by six forces — favorable collective sum → higher profit potential
1
Barriers to Entry
✓ High — blocks new competitors from entering
✗ Low — easy market entry floods supply
2
Barriers to Exit
✓ Low — weak competitors can leave; market clears
✗ High — weak firms stay and destroy margins for all
3
Customer Buying Power
✓ Low — buyers accept prices; limited negotiating leverage
✗ High — buyers force price concessions; squeeze margins
4
Supplier Selling Power
✓ Low — suppliers cannot impose costs; competitive inputs
✗ High — suppliers control input costs; extract value
5
Product Substitutes
✓ Few / None — customers cannot switch; captive demand
✗ Many — customers have alternatives; limits pricing power
6
Competitive Rivalry
✓ Low — stable margins; disciplined competitors
✗ Intense — price wars, high costs, margin erosion
