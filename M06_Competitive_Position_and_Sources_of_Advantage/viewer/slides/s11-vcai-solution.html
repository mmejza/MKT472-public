VCAI: Solution
Aerospace Fastener Manufacturer — VCAI = 89.7 → Cost Advantage
Firm Competitor A Competitor B Competitor C
COGS (% of sales) 52% 58% 61% 55%
Avg. Competitor COGS — (58+61+55) ÷ 3 = 58.0%
VCAI 89.7 52 ÷ 58.0 × 100 = 89.7
VCAI = 89.7 → COST ADVANTAGE
Index BELOW 100: firm spends fewer cents per dollar of revenue than the average competitor. This is a cost advantage.
Strategic Interpretation

A VCAI of 89.7 means this firm can price at or below competitors and still maintain better margins — or use the cost savings to fund differentiation investment.

Strategic question: Should this firm use its VCAI advantage to lower prices (gain share) or hold prices (expand margins)? The answer depends on its RMS and IAI.
