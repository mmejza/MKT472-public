Competitive Advantage
Three types — commit to one; pursuing all three simultaneously produces parity on all
Cost Advantage
· Lower variable costs
· Lower marketing expenses
· Lower operating expenses
Result
Same or better margins at lower prices
Differentiation Advantage
· Product differentiation
· Service quality
· Brand reputation
Result
Above-average performance at premium prices
Marketing Advantage
· Market share leadership
· Product line breadth
· Channel dominance
Result
Brand + distribution drive above-avg ROI
Core LO1 Argument

Committing to ONE source — and investing to make it substantial, sustainable, and leverageable — drives above-average ROI. Firms that split investment across all three achieve parity on none.
