Channel Advantage and Market Share
Distributor outlet share → market share — a structural advantage independent of product quality
Mechanism — Four Steps
1
Exclusive access to top-tier distributors controls market access
2
As distributor outlet share rises 20% → 40%, market share accelerates at an increasing rate
3
Above 40% outlet share, market share continues rising but at a decelerating rate
4
This advantage is independent of cost or differentiation — it is structural

Strategic Implication

A firm that controls the best distributors makes it structurally difficult for competitors to access customers — even if those competitors have better products or lower prices.
