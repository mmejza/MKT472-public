Reading the Three Indices Together
Combining Product, Service, and Brand scores reveals where to invest — and where you've hit ceiling
Product Diff. Index
> 0
Firm has genuine performance advantage on key customer benefits — invest to defend and widen this gap
Service Quality Index
= 0
Parity — no service advantage to defend or exploit. Service is not hurting the firm, but it is not helping either.
Brand Advantage Index
= 0
Parity — brand is not a differentiator at current ratings. Both brand dimensions are within the threshold.
All Three Together
Identifies which dimension to invest in (where a gap exists) and which is already at ceiling — where additional investment earns no more advantage points
Strategic Reveal

Three index scores together tell a firm: where its competitive advantage actually lives, which dimensions are at parity (investable), and which are near ceiling (not worth additional spend).
