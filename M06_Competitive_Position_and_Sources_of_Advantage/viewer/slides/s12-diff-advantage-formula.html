Differentiation Advantage Index
Importance-weighted sum of advantage points — only differences ≥ 2.0 count
Diff. Index = Σ (Importance Weight × Advantage Points per Benefit)
Components
Importance Weight — customer-stated relative importance of each benefit (sums to 100)
Advantage Points — earned only when performance difference ≥ 2.0
Advantage = Firm Rating − Competitor Rating
Firm − Competitor Diff.Advantage Points Earned
≥ +2.0Earn the full importance weight (+)
Between −2.0 and +2.0Zero — difference not meaningful
≤ −2.0Lose the full importance weight (−)
Threshold Trap

Outperforming a competitor by less than 2.0 points earns ZERO advantage — the difference is not strategically meaningful to customers. A score of +1.9 and a score of −1.9 both contribute zero to the index.
