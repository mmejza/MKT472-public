Economic Value Calculation — Solution
EV = crash savings − operating cost penalty
Cost Component Next-Best Firm's Product Difference
Annual operating cost ($10 vs $15 × 2,500 hr) $25,000 $37,500 +$12,500

Total Annual TCO $45,000 $38,500 −$6,500
Economic Value

EV = $45,000 − $38,500 = $6,500 / year
This is the maximum annual premium the customer can pay above the next-best alternative.

Answer: $2.20 per unit

Dividing by usage units, the textbook derives a per-unit price ceiling of $2.20. This is not the target price — it is the maximum justifiable premium.

Key Takeaway

The firm's product costs more to operate but delivers far greater crash risk reduction. EV captures this trade-off in a single monetary figure. Without EV analysis, the firm cannot defend a price premium above the competitor's price.
