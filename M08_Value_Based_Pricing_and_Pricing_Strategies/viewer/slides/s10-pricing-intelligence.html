Pricing Intelligence Requirements
What each approach demands — and what it ignores
Pricing Approach Competitor Intelligence Customer Intelligence Starting Point
Cost-Based None None Internal costs + desired margin
Competitor-Reactive Extensive None Market price benchmarks
Value-Based Extensive Extensive Customer economic value vs. next-best
Customer-Reactive Extensive Extensive Segment willingness-to-pay
Strategic Conclusion

Cost-based pricing requires the least information and produces the least precise price. Value-based pricing requires the most — but anchors price to what customers will actually pay. Higher intelligence cost, higher margin ceiling.
