Decision Rule — Synthesis

If |elasticity| < |BE%|, the price change is profitable. For a price increase: if the market loses fewer customers than the BE% threshold, raise the price. For the price decrease example (BE% = 33.3%), you need elasticity stronger than −3.3 to break even — rarely achieved in most markets.
