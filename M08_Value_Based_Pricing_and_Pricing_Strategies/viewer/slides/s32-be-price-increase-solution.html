BE%: Price Increase — Solution
Volume can fall up to 20% — market share from 2.0% to 1.6% — and still break even
Calculation

BE% = −(+$10 − $0) ÷ ($40 + $10 − $0) = −$10 ÷ $50 = −20.0%

Strategic Interpretation

If actual volume loss from the 10% price increase is less than 20%, the price increase is profitable. In most markets with price elasticity between 0 and −2.0, this threshold is rarely breached — making a price increase the high-probability correct move.
