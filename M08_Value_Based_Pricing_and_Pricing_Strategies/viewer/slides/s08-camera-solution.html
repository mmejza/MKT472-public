The Question Cost-Based Ignores

What if a different price captures more total profit — not just margin per unit?

Key Insight

Price affects volume → volume affects unit cost → unit cost changes the very margin used to justify the price. Cost-based pricing is self-referential. It does not account for the demand its own price creates.
