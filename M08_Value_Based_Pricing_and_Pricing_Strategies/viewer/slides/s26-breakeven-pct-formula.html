Break-Even % Analysis
How much must volume change to offset a price or cost change?
BE% = −(ΔP − Δvc) ÷ (CM + ΔP − Δvc)

ΔP

Change in price per unit. Positive = price increase. Negative = price decrease.

Δvc

Change in variable cost per unit. Positive = cost increase. Negative = cost decrease.

CM

Current contribution margin per unit = Price − Variable Cost

Direction Rule — Price Decrease

BE% is positive → volume must rise by at least that % to break even. If actual volume gain < BE%, do not cut price.

Direction Rule — Price Increase

BE% is negative → volume can fall by up to that % and still break even. If actual volume loss < BE%, raise price.
