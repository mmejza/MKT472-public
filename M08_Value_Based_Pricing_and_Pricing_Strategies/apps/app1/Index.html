MKT 472 · Module 8 · App 1

Value-in-Use / Economic Value Calculator

Compare purchase price vs. total cost of ownership and test whether a firm premium is economically justified.

Learning Goal

Evaluate whether a higher purchase price is justified by total ownership savings over time. Compare annual and discounted economic value across firm and competitor offers. Interpret price premium decisions using EV-based metrics instead of price alone.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Base Case.

How to Use This App
  1. Load a preset or enter competitor and firm costs for each ownership component.
  2. Set product life, discount rate, and annual volume to match the quiz scenario.
  3. Review the ownership table and summary metrics, including NPV of savings.
  4. Use the verdict and cost chart to judge whether the firm premium is justified.
