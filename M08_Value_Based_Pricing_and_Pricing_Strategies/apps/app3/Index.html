Break-Even Pricing Calculator

Calculate required break-even volume change, compare against elasticity-based expected volume change, and evaluate strategy viability.

Learning Goal

Evaluate whether price-change strategies can maintain or improve gross profit under realistic volume responses. Compare required break-even volume change with elasticity-based expected change. Interpret strategy viability using both contribution logic and demand response.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Price Cut Scenario.

How to Use This App
  1. Load a scenario preset or enter pricing, cost, and volume inputs manually.
  2. Use Tab A to calculate break-even volume change requirements.
  3. Use Tab B to estimate expected volume change from elasticity and classify demand response.
  4. Compare required versus expected volume change in the final comparison panel and gauge.
