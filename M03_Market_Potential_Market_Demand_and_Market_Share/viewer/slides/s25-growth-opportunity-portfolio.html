Bottom Rule — Read MDI and SDI Together

MDI alone identifies whether the market is underdeveloped. SDI alone identifies whether the firm's share is underdeveloped. Only by reading both simultaneously can you identify which of the two investment types will produce the highest return. This is the core diagnostic skill for the Vantara Outdoors case.
