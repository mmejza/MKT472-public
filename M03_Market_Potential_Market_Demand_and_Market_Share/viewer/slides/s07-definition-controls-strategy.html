Risk: Strategic Myopia

Strategic Rule

Your market definition determines which opportunities you can see — and which threats you cannot. Before calculating MDI, MSI, or SDI, confirm the market is defined at the right scope. Calibration matters more than precision.

Coming next: Once the market is defined, Market Potential measures the ceiling. MDI reveals how much of that ceiling has been reached.
