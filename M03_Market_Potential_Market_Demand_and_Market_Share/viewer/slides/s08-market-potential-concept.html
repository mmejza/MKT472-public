What Is Market Potential?
The maximum demand achievable if all barriers to adoption were removed
Market Potential
Maximum possible demand given the current population of potential customers — assuming awareness is perfect, price is acceptable, product is available, and all other barriers are removed.
Market Demand
Actual total purchases across all buyers in the market during a defined period — what is happening, not what could happen.
Key Distinction
Potential = ceiling (rarely reached)
Demand = current floor
Gap = unrealized opportunity
Why It Matters
A firm cannot outgrow its market potential — only redefine the market. Knowing the ceiling tells you when to shift from market development to share competition.
Strategic Implication

Market Potential is not a forecast — it is a structural ceiling. MDI measures how far the market has traveled toward that ceiling. Low MDI = large untapped potential = invest in market development.
