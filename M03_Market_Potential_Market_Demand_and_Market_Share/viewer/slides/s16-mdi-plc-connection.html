KEY INSIGHT — NMC Peaks in Late Growth
Net Marketing Contribution peaks before demand peaks. The most profitable stage is late growth — when volume is high but competitive intensity has not yet compressed margins. Waiting for maturity to invest means missing the profit window. [Gate 1 — Ch. 3, pp. 97–98]
