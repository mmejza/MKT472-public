Volume Sold: Formula and Worked Example
Current and projected volume — market growth and share gain operate simultaneously
Current: Demand × Share  |  Projected: Demand × (1 + Growth) × New Share

Critical Calculation Step

For projected volume: apply market growth to get the new market demand first, then multiply by the new share percentage. Applying the new share to the old market demand understates projected volume. The order of operations matters.

Volume Attribution: +0.48M gain = market effect (+0.36M from demand growth at constant 18%) + share effect (+0.12M from gaining 2 share points in the growing market). Both forces contributed.
