Market Share Performance Tree Builder

Build MSI, SPI, and SDI, then map product position in the Growth Opportunity Portfolio.

Learning Goal

Use share-tree conversion stages to diagnose leakage and quantify MSI, SPI, and SDI performance. Connect SDI with MDI to interpret portfolio quadrant position and strategy direction.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Textbook Example.

How to Use This App
  1. Choose a preset or adjust current and desired stage sliders.
  2. Review MSI, SPI, SDI, leakage bars, and share tree in Tab A outputs.
  3. Use Tab B to enter MDI and evaluate portfolio quadrant placement.
  4. Use the gap summary and prescription to interpret improvement priorities.
