Market Potential Estimator

Estimate market potential and compare outcomes across market definitions.

Learning Goal

Use market potential inputs to see how definition choices affect potential size, measured demand, and MDI interpretation. Identify how the same category can look very different under narrow versus broad scope.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Narrow Definition.

How to Use This App
  1. Choose a preset, then review which inputs are available for that scenario.
  2. Enter or adjust input values and use the growth slider as needed.
  3. Read the output cards for market potential, MDI, and share-point value.
  4. Use the projection table to inspect Year 1 through Year 3 demand changes.
