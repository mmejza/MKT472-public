Product Life-Cycle Stage Diagnostor

Diagnose PLC stage from observable indicators to interpret demand, GP, and NMC timing patterns.

Learning Goal

Use market indicators to identify the most likely PLC stage and evaluate confidence in the diagnosis. Connect stage conditions to strategy guidance and timing differences between NMC and demand peaks.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Introduction.

How to Use This App
  1. Choose a preset or adjust sliders and trend selectors to match the scenario.
  2. Review diagnosed stage and confidence after each input change.
  3. Use the strategy card and indicator summary table to check stage consistency.
  4. Interpret the trajectory chart and optional GP overlay for timing relationships.
