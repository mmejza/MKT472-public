Method 1 — Formula
CLV = [M × (r ÷ (1 + i − r))] − AC
= [$61 × (0.80 ÷ (1.10 − 0.80))] − $51
= [$61 × (0.80 ÷ 0.30)] − $51
= [$61 × 2.667] − $51
= $162.67 − $51

Common Error — Denominator

The denominator is (1 + i − r), not (1 + i + r) and not (i − r). With i=10% and r=80%: 1 + 0.10 − 0.80 = 0.30. A small denominator means high CLV — which is why high retention customers are so valuable.
