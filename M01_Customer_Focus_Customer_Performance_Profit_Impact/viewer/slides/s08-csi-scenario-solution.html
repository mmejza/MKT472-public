Why the Company Is Worse Off

Removing the bottom tiers shifts weight toward Somewhat Satisfied — not toward Very Satisfied. The distribution becomes more concentrated at a low-profit tier, pulling the average down. Averages hide what distributions reveal.

The Right Strategy

To improve average profit, the firm must invest in moving customers up to Very Satisfied — not simply eliminating the bottom. An 8-point CSI increase (72→80) would raise average profit significantly. See Ch. 1 for the calculation.
