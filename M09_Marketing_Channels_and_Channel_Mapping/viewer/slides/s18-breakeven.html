Break-Even Market Share

Formula BE Share (%) = [ M&S Expense ($) ÷ (Pocket Price − COGS) ] ÷ Market Demand × 100
M&S Expense ($)
Total marketing and sales spend required by the channel strategy — not a percentage
PP − COGS
Margin per unit — what each unit contributes to covering M&S fixed costs
Market Demand
Total market in units — converts volume to share percentage
