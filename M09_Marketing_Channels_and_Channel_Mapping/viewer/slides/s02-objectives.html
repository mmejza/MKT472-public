APPLICATION
Trace the pocket price waterfall across a multi-tier channel — deducting retailer discounts, distributor discounts, commissions, and transaction costs — to compute what the manufacturer actually receives per unit sold.

Calculate NMC, Marketing ROS, and Marketing ROI for a channel strategy; identify why the channel with the highest margin and NMC can simultaneously have the lowest ROI.
LO3
EVALUATION
Evaluate whether switching channel strategy is financially justified by computing break-even market share and comparing it to current share; assess the risk of each channel option.
LO4
