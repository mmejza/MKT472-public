Business-to-consumer channel options

Direct sales
Company-employed sales force contacts consumer directly. Highest cost per contact; highest communication quality.Mary Kay, Electrolux, Amway — sales distributors are the de facto sales force
Digital
Web-based ordering and fulfillment. Lowest variable cost; highest reach potential; transforms small-customer economics.Dow Chemical e-epoxy.com: attracted 1,400 new visitors in 7 weeks; ⅔ of orders from new customers
Direct mktg
Catalogs, mail, telemarketing. Less expensive than sales force; more limited sales communication.L.L. Bean, Eddie Bauer — database-driven catalog marketing at scale
Wholesale → Retail
Traditional indirect B2C structure. Widest reach; lowest manufacturer control; pocket price reduced by multiple discount layers.Consumer packaged goods: manufacturer → wholesaler → retailer → consumer
Online retail
Retailer buys from manufacturer/wholesaler and resells online. Works best for standard products customers don't need to inspect before purchase.Books, electronics, software — Amazon as indirect online channel
