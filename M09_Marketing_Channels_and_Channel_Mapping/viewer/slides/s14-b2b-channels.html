MKT 472 · M09 Channel Types: B2B LO4

Business-to-business channel options — only 25% of industrial sales are direct

Direct sales
Company sales force. Best for large accounts where communication value justifies high cost ($100–$300 per call).Microsoft/HP direct sales teams for Fortune 500 enterprise accounts
Agents / Reps
Independent commissioned representatives. Sell on behalf of manufacturer; paid only on results; no base salary cost.50% of industrial B2B sales flow through agent + distributor combinations
Industrial distributors
Take ownership of product; handle physical distribution and delivery. Reduce manufacturer's inventory and logistics burden.Welding rod manufacturer: 500 distributors across 1,500 locations serving 6 market segments
VARs
Bundle components from multiple manufacturers into customized end-user systems. Chapter-exclusive detail — tested in Gate 1.Agricultural VAR: computers + software + telecom = full farm management system
OEMs
Manufacture new products from purchased components. Chapter-exclusive distinction from VARs — tested in Gate 1.Ford (buys tires, body parts → manufactures vehicles); IBM (buys Seagate drives, Intel chips → builds computers)
