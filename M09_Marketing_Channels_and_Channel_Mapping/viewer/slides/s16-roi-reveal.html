Why ROI = NMC ÷ M&S Expense. Direct: $2,075 ÷ $1,425 = 146%. Indirect: $140 ÷ $34 = 415%. The indirect channel's M&S expense is 24× smaller than direct — so each dollar invested returns nearly 3× more, despite the much smaller absolute NMC.
Correct rule High margin + high NMC ≠ high ROI. ROI is determined by how efficiently M&S investment generates profit — not by margin or absolute profit level.
