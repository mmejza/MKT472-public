Channel Pocket Price & Profitability Calculator

MKT 472 · M09 · App 1

Compare channel pocket price and profitability tradeoffs across Direct, Mixed, and Indirect structures.

Learning Goal

Compare channel profitability outcomes using pocket price, NMC, ROS, and ROI metrics. Evaluate how channel structure changes can shift ranking between NMC and ROI results. Interpret break-even share and margin-of-safety outputs for channel viability decisions.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Base Case.

How to Use This App
  1. Choose a preset or adjust channel inputs for Direct, Mixed, and Indirect columns.
  2. Review waterfall steps to confirm channel pocket price formation.
  3. Compare profitability cards and aggregate metrics to evaluate channel tradeoffs.
  4. Use chart and break-even bars to assess ROI, NMC, and margin-of-safety implications.
