Channel Strategy Comparator

MKT 472 · M09 · App 2 | Complete App 1 before using App 2

Evaluate channel strategy profitability tradeoffs across market-share scenarios using the calculator and chart outputs.

Learning Goal

Compare how Indirect, Mixed, and Direct channel strategies perform as market share changes. Identify when higher net margin contribution aligns with lower marketing ROI because of differing M&S expense levels.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Indirect (Retailer-Wholesale).

How to Use This App
  1. Select a strategy, adjust market demand, and set current and target market-share values.
  2. Enable the M&S override only when your quiz prompt specifies a custom expense value.
  3. Review NMC, ROI, ROS, break-even share, and margin-of-safety outputs.
  4. Use the chart to compare how strategy NMC changes from 0% to 40% market share.
