Needs-Based vs. Demographic-First
Sequence determines whether segments are actionable — needs first is not optional
✓ Needs-Based (Correct)
1
Group customers by similar NEEDS
2
Find demographics that distinguish the groups
→
Result: Segments are differentiable — respond differently to different programs
→
Guidance: Clear product development and message strategy
✗ Demographic-First (The Trap)
1
Group customers by age / income / SIC code
2
Try to infer their needs from demographic data
→
Result: Segments may share identical needs despite demographic differences
→
Problem: 3 categories × 6 variables = 729 combinations, most irrelevant
Takeaway

Demographics identify WHO customers are. Needs identify WHAT they require. Always start with needs — demographics confirm and profile the groups you have already identified.
