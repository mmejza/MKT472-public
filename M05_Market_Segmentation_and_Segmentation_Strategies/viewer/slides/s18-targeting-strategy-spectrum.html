Six Targeting Strategies: Full Spectrum
Strategy choice depends on resource availability, competitive structure, and need heterogeneity
1 · Mass Market
Needs are homogeneous, OR cost of serving segments exceeds benefit
Walmart
2 · Large Segment
One segment dominates demand; resources focused for maximum penetration
Chevy Truck
3 · Adjacent Segment
Full penetration of core segment; expand to overlapping adjacent segments incrementally
Toyota: Corona → Corolla → Camry → Lexus
4 · Multi-Segment
Resources support distinct programs for multiple segments; combined NMC exceeds single-segment
Gas stations: Road Warriors + Gen F3 + True Blues
5 · Small / Niche
Limited resources, very specific capability-segment fit; dominant share within a narrow segment
Sub-Zero: 70% of super-premium refrigerators
6 · Subsegment
Core needs met; meaningful differences remain within segment that justify tailored programs
James Hardie ColorPlus (within Quality segment)
