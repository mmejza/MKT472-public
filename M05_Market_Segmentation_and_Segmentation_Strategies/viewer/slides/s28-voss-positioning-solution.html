Voss Water: Positioning Analysis Solution
All four elements present — value proposition is the weakest link
Element Present in Statement? Strength Assessment
Target Market Yes Affluent, trend-conscious urban professionals — reasonably specific to a needs-based group
Competitive Set Yes Luxury bottled water — correctly frames the comparison category
Value Proposition Yes — but weak "Ultra-pure, pristine taste and iconic aesthetic" — qualitative, unverifiable, copyable by any competitor
Reasons to Believe Yes Artesian aquifer + Norwegian origin — brand story, but does not verify purity performance
How to Strengthen the Value Proposition

Replace qualitative language with measurable outcomes: "lowest TDS (total dissolved solids) of any major bottled water brand at [X] ppm" or "independently tested [Y]× lower contaminant concentration than [Competitor]." Quantified purity is verifiable; "pristine taste" is not — and a competitor can copy the words without copying the water.
