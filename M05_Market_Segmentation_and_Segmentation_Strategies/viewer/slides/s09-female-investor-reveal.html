Key Insight

Demographics (age, income, education) are OUTPUTS of the process — they confirm and identify needs-based groups, not create them. Notice: Career Women and Single Parents overlap in age range (35–55) but have dramatically different income profiles and opposite investment attitudes. Demographic-first segmentation would have grouped them together.
