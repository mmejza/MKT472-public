Gasoline Station: Multi-Segment Strategy
59% of customers generate the highest revenue — mass-market strategy serves the other 41%
Segment Size Revenue per Customer Core Need Strategy
Road Warriors 16% High Premium fuel, services, snacks en route Target
Gen F3 27% High Convenience, speed, quality food options Target
True Blues 16% Moderate–High Brand loyalty, consistent quality Target
Homebodies 21% Moderate Local station, routine fill-up Serve if reached
Price Shoppers 20% Low Lowest price only Do not target
Strategy Shift

Before: Mass-market strategy built around price → attracts Price Shoppers (lowest revenue, 20% of customers).
After: Multi-segment targeting Road Warriors + Gen F3 + True Blues → 59% of customers, highest revenue per visit. Repositioning away from price breaks the mass-market trap.
