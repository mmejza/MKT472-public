Segment Profitability: NMC and Marketing ROI
Step 4 of the 7-step process — calculate profitability at a target market share level
Net Marketing Contribution (NMC)
NMC = (Demand × Share × Price × Margin%) − (M&S% × Revenue)
where Revenue = Demand × Share × Price
Marketing Return on Sales (ROS)
Marketing ROS = NMC ÷ Revenue × 100
Marketing Return on Investment (ROI)
Marketing ROI = NMC ÷ M&S Expense × 100
NMC vs. ROI Can Diverge

A segment can produce higher NMC but lower ROI than another segment. Resource-constrained firms must use Marketing ROI to allocate efficiently — NMC alone overstates the case for high-cost segments. See Ch. 5 Fig. 5-12 (silicon sealants) for the full worked comparison.
