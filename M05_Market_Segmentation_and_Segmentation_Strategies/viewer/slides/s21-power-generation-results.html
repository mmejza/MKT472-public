Context: 7,000 customers across 12 distinct need types. Before: one marketing program for all customers. After: 12 tailored programs, one per need-based segment. Market conditions comparable across all three regions.
Evidence

The mass-market control region (Region C) gained only 3% in the same declining market where multi-segment regions gained 12–18%. Tailoring programs to distinct customer needs, not market conditions, drives the performance gap.
