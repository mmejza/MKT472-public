Why Size Misleads

The large segment's 31 competitors compress share (4%) and price ($42). The smaller segment's 3 competitors allow 18% share and a $185 premium price. NMC is 8.6× higher in the smaller segment. Applied to Apex Athletic: Segment C (14M Value Seekers) is the worst target despite being the largest.
