Positioning Statement Template
Step 5 of the 7-step process — four required elements, each with a distinct role
"For [TARGET MARKET] who [NEED / PROBLEM], [BRAND] is [COMPETITIVE SET] that [VALUE PROPOSITION], because [REASONS TO BELIEVE]."
Target Market
The precisely defined needs-based segment from Steps 1–2. Not a demographic group — a group defined by shared needs and benefits sought.
Competitive Set
Defines what the brand is being compared to — and what "better than" means. Sets the frame of reference for the value proposition.
Value Proposition
The specific, quantified benefit the target customer receives — not generic language. Must be differentiating and meaningful to the segment.
Reasons to Believe
Credible, verifiable evidence that the value proposition claim is true. Third-party testing, data, certifications — not just assertions.
