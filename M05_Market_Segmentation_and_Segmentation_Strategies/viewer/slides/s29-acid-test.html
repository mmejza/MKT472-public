Segment Acid Test
Step 6 — verify positioning statements produce correct self-selection before full investment
1
Create Storyboards
Develop one visual storyboard per segment, each showing the segment's distinct value proposition — no brand names revealed
2
Recruit Target Customers
Classify recruits into segments using needs-based screening questions — not demographics
3
Present Storyboards, Request Self-Selection
Show each customer all storyboards; ask which one describes a product they would purchase
4
Measure Self-Selection Accuracy
≥70% correct self-selection → segment is viable, advance to Step 7
Below 70% → value proposition is not differentiated enough; revise and retest
Gate 1 — Ch. 5, pp. 172–173
The Acid Test prevents committing full marketing mix investment to segments with indistinguishable positioning. Low accuracy is a diagnosis, not a failure — it signals where the value proposition needs sharper differentiation.
