The 7-Step Needs-Based Segmentation Process
Each step feeds the next — the process is sequential, not iterative at the start
1
Needs-Based Segmentation Group customers by similar needs and benefits sought
2
Segment Identification Find the demographics, lifestyle, and usage behaviors that distinguish each group
3
Segment Attractiveness Score each segment: market demand · competitive intensity · market access
4
Segment Profitability Calculate NMC and Marketing ROI for each segment at a target share level
5
Segment Positioning Develop a value proposition and product-price positioning for each segment
6
Segment Acid Test Present storyboards to target customers; verify correct self-selection into segments
7
Marketing Mix Strategy Expand positioning into full product, price, promotion, and place strategy
