Service Quality Dimensions
Five dimensions — each can be a differentiator or a disqualifier
1
Reliability
Ability to perform the promised service dependably and accurately — every time, at every location
2
Responsiveness
Willingness to help customers and provide prompt service — speed and availability of assistance
3
Assurance
Knowledge and courtesy of employees; ability to convey trust and confidence
4
Empathy
Caring, individualized attention given to customers — treating each customer as a person, not a transaction
5
Tangibles
Physical evidence of service quality: facilities, equipment, personnel appearance, communication materials
