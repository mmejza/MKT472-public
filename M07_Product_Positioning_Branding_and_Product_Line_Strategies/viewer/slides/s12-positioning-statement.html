Positioning Statement: Components and Template
Review from M05 — four required components; one mandatory structure
Template

For [target market], Brand X is the only brand among all [competitive set] that offers [unique value claim] because [reasons to believe].

Example — Voss Water

For upscale consumers who make a design statement with their choice of water, Voss is the only brand among all bottled waters that offers the purest and most distinctive drinking experience because it derives from an artesian source in southern Norway and is packaged in a stylish, iconic glass bottle.

Unique Value Claim Examples
Tesla Solar — "Lowest Cost Solar Panels in America — Money-back guarantee"
Peet's Coffee — "The Original Craft Coffee"
What Makes a Claim "Unique"

A UVC is specific, defensible, and competitively exclusive. "High quality" is not a UVC — it is generic. A UVC must be something competitors cannot credibly claim.
