Product Positioning: The Core Question
Review from M05 — not re-tested in Gate 1; required context for differentiation
The Positioning Question

"Buyers in the target market should buy our product rather than others being offered and used because: ___________."

To Whom?
Who is the specific target market? Define the segment precisely enough that the positioning can be tailored to their needs.
Why Should They Buy?
What is the unique value claim? What specific benefit does this product offer that competitors cannot match?
How to Make the Claim?
What evidence, proof points, or reasons-to-believe make the claim credible and defensible?
What Positioning Is Not

A positioning statement is strategic — written for an internal managerial audience. It is not a brand slogan, a tagline, or an advertising headline. Positioning defines the decision; the slogan communicates the decision externally.
