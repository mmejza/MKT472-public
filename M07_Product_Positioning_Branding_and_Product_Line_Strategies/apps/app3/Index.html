MKT 472 · Module 7 · App 3

Product Line Strategy Analyzer

Analyze hardcoded ROA benchmarks and classify brand growth strategy decisions.

Learning Goal

Evaluate how lifecycle stage and line breadth combinations affect hardcoded ROA outcomes. Interpret revenue effects of ROA changes under different preset scenarios. Classify brand growth decisions by mapping input conditions to line extension, brand extension, fighter/flanker, or new brand outputs.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Apex Athletic.

How to Use This App
  1. Choose a named preset or set before/after lifecycle stage and line breadth values manually.
  2. Review hardcoded ROA outputs, ROA change, and revenue impact for the selected before/after states.
  3. Use Tab B inputs to classify the brand growth strategy and review logic notes.
  4. Check warnings and QA indicators for counterintuitive or ambiguous classification cases.
