MKT 472 · Module 7 · App 1

Positioning Mapper

Classify Customer Value Hierarchy (CVH) and analyze POPs/PODs for two brands.

Learning Goal

Classify product attributes within the Customer Value Hierarchy and compare brand positioning using POP/POD analysis. Distinguish baseline category requirements from differentiating claims. Use the outputs to explain how brand position is built and defended.

Worksheet Quiz Instructions

  1. Read the Canvas quiz question first.
  2. Follow the preset, scenario, or modifications specified in the question.
  3. Use this app to perform the required calculations and analysis.
  4. Enter your answers in the Canvas quiz.
  5. Answers are submitted through Canvas, not through this app.

Required for Quiz

The Canvas quiz question determines which preset, scenario, or modifications to use. If a question does not specify otherwise, begin with the default preset shown in this app. Default preset: Electric Vehicle.

How to Use This App
  1. Choose a product preset and classify attributes into CVH levels by dragging or selecting levels.
  2. Click Check My Classification to evaluate accepted CVH assignments and explanations.
  3. Switch to the POP/POD tab and enter brand names with three attributes per brand.
  4. Run analysis to view Venn outputs and refine the editable positioning-gap summary.
