Brand Personality Scale: Five Types and Defining Traits
Rate each trait 1–5 — the profile reveals which personality dimension the brand expresses
Sincerity
Down-to-Earth · Honest · Wholesome · Cheerful
Excitement
Daring · Spirited · Imaginative · Up-to-Date
Competence
Reliable · Intelligent · Successful
Sophistication
Upper Class · Charming · Sophisticated
Ruggedness
Rugged · Outdoorsy · Tough
Two Things to Notice

1. Traits within each personality type are not synonyms — they specify different facets of that personality. Sincerity includes Cheerful; Excitement includes Up-to-Date; these distinctions matter for classification.
2. When competing products meet rational needs equally, the brand with the personality that best matches the target customer's psychological needs wins.
