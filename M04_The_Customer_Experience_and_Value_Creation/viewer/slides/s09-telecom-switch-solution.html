Higher purchase price — lower total lifecycle cost — positive economic value

Key Takeaway

A higher price ≠ inferior value. Compare total lifecycle costs, not purchase prices. At 100 units, the $75 price premium costs $7,500 — but the buyer saves $12,500. Net gain: $5,000.
