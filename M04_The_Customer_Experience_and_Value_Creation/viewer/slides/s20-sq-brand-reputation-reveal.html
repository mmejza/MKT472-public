Service Quality and Brand Reputation Indexes
Same method, same threshold rule — service quality is the competitive vulnerability
Service Quality Index: 90
Total Relative Advantage = −10
Index = 100 + (−10) = 90

Interpretation: 10% below competitive average on service quality. Despite strong product performance (137), the firm is at a service disadvantage. Competitors outperform on service response and support reliability.
Brand Reputation Index: 140
Total Relative Advantage = +40
Index = 100 + 40 = 140

Interpretation: 40% above competitive average on brand reputation. Customers perceive this brand as significantly more credible and trustworthy than competitors. A strong brand can offset a service weakness at the overall performance level.

Setup for PCV Index

All three performance indexes and their weights feed the Overall Performance Index, which then enters the PCV calculation on the next slide. Weighted average: (137 × 0.60) + (90 × 0.30) + (140 × 0.10) = 123.
