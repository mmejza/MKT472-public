Four Types of Customer Value
Each type requires a different measurement method — confusing types produces wrong results
Economic Value
LO2 — slides + chapter
Cost savings over the product's full lifecycle — measured objectively from market data
Economic Value = Competitor LCC − Company LCC
Relative Performance Value
LO3 — slides + chapter
Performance advantage relative to competing products — fair price derived from price-performance relationship
Customer Value = Fair Price − Actual Price
Perceived Customer Value
LO4 — slides + chapter
Customer perceptions of benefits minus cost of purchase — requires direct customer measurement
PCV Index = Overall Performance Index − Cost of Purchase Index
Emotional Value
LO1 — chapter-only detail
Brand personality that resonates with psychological needs — primary differentiator when economic and performance value are equal
