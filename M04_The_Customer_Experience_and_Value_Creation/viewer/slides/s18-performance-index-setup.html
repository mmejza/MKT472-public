Threshold Rule — Learn This Exactly

Business outperforms a competitor by >1 point (i.e., ≥2 points) → earn full importance points for that competitor pairing.
Difference of ≤1 point, OR competitor wins → earn zero or negative importance points.
This is NOT partial credit. The threshold is binary.
