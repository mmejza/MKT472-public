How Importance is Derived

Importance % = attribute range ÷ sum of all ranges. Price's range (34) ÷ 100 = 34%. No direct survey of weights was needed — builders revealed importance through their bundle rankings. This is conjoint's key advantage: revealed preferences under tradeoffs are more reliable than stated importance ratings.
