Brand Personality: Classify Three Novel Scenarios
Identify the personality type from the description — use defining traits, not named brand recognition
Brand X → Sincerity Down-to-Earth · Honest · Wholesome
A personal care brand features everyday people in unretouched photographs and uses messaging centered on honesty, real beauty, and genuine self-acceptance. Its promise: "We see you as you are."
Brand Y → Competence Reliable · Intelligent · Successful
A technology company's products are trusted by professionals and consumers alike for dependable performance, intelligent design, and consistent success across its entire ecosystem.
Brand Z → Sophistication Upper Class · Charming · Sophisticated
A luxury goods brand sells through limited-access boutiques, uses no mass advertising, and prices at a level that signals exclusive status. Its packaging color alone signals the brand.
Skill Transfer

Each answer was derivable from the defining traits, not from brand name recognition. Gate 1 will present scenarios like these — not the named examples (Betty Crocker, Nike, HP, Lexus, Timberland). The trait-to-scenario matching skill is what Gate 1 tests.
