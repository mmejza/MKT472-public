Conjoint's Critical Advantage

Builders ranked nine product bundles — never stating importance weights directly. The 34%/28%/19%/19% percentages were derived from those rankings. This reveals true tradeoff preferences: when forced to choose between bundles, what do builders actually sacrifice? Direct surveys produce stated preferences; conjoint reveals revealed preferences.
