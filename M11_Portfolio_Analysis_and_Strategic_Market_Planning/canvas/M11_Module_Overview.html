Central Question — Module 11
Does current performance predict strategic priority?
The data you will analyze shows a product line with the highest Marketing ROS (32%) and ROI (320%) in the portfolio. The instinct is to protect it — manage defensively, preserve the margin, do not disturb what is working. This module argues that instinct is wrong in this specific case — and makes you prove why. The answer requires two index calculations, one grid placement, and a three-year financial projection. By the time you finish, you will have a defensible position. Whether it matches the instinct is the point of the exercise.

Why two gates instead of one
A single pre-lecture quiz creates one weakness: students who score just above the threshold proceed without genuine chapter internalization. The two-gate model closes this gap. Gate 1 certifies you read before viewing the slides. Gate 2 certifies you synthesized reading with slides before viewing the lecture. Students who game Gate 1 cannot game Gate 2 — the applied questions require both sources.
