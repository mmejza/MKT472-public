Strategic Market Planning
LO 2 Concept Definition 04 / 21

A process in which firms develop marketing strategies for sustaining and improving short- and long-run performance across their portfolio of products and markets. Portfolio analysis drives it; strategy selection follows from it.

3–5 year horizon
Offensive output
  • Grow sales in existing markets
  • Improve competitive position
  • Enter new attractive markets
Defensive output
  • Protect or optimize position
  • Monetize for maximum cash flow
  • Harvest or divest
Without this process, resource allocation is intuition — not analysis. A firm cannot know which products to invest in and which to harvest without computing both dimensions.
