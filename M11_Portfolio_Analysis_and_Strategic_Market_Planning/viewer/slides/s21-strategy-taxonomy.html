Strategic Market Plans — Seven Strategies
LO 5 · 6 Classification 21 / 21
Offensive — Attractive markets / Improvable position
Invest to Grow Invest marketing and sales resources to grow market demand or grow market share in an expanding market
Improve Position Strengthen competitive position in an attractive segment where the firm is currently underperforming its potential
New Market Entry Enter new attractive markets or develop entirely new product-markets; requires large upfront investment, operates at a loss until break-even
Defensive — Mature markets / Established position
Protect Position Protect market share and competitive advantage in a market the firm dominates; invest to hold, not to grow
Optimize Position Maximize marketing profit at late-growth or mature stage; optimize price-volume mix, reduce unnecessary marketing spend
Monetize Manage for maximum cash flow in a less attractive market; limit investment, remain in market
Harvest / Divest Extract short-run profit while exiting (harvest = managed exit; divest = sell or close immediately)
