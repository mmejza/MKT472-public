MAI Rating Scale

0 = Very Unattractive market
100 = Very Attractive market

CPI Rating Scale

0 = Considerably Behind competitors
100 = Considerably Ahead of competitors
Measures standing vs. rivals

Same 0–100 range. Completely different meaning. A rating of 40 in MAI = "somewhat unattractive market." A rating of 40 in CPI = "somewhat behind competitors."
