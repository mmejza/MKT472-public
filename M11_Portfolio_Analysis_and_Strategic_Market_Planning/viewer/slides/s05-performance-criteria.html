Business Performance Criteria for Portfolio Review
LO 1 Classification 05 / 21
Market Performance
  • Market share position
  • Sales growth — unit volume
  • Sales growth — dollar volume
  • Customer satisfaction / retention
Marketing Profitability
  • Net Marketing Contribution (NMC)
  • Marketing Return on Sales (ROS)
  • Marketing Return on Investment (ROI)
  • Gross profit by segment
Traditional Financial Metrics (also required)
Revenue · Gross Profit · Operating Profit · Operating Profit %
Rule
Financial metrics alone are backward-looking — they report what happened. Market + marketing profitability metrics reveal competitive trajectory: where the firm is heading, not just where it has been.
