Meridian Industrial — Adding the Life Cycle Changes Everything
LO 1 Reveal 08 / 21

A portfolio weighted to mature markets looks stable today. It is a revenue cliff in 3–5 years.

Product Stage Sales ($M) % Revenue Strategic Reality
Alpha Mature $180 45% Slow-declining market; margin protection priority
Beta Mature $120 30% Near-zero growth; harvest candidate within 3 years
Gamma Late Growth $60 15% Future profit engine — requires investment now
Delta Mature $30 8% Optimize or harvest; limited upside
Epsilon Declining $10 2% Exit candidate
Key reveal
No products in Emerging or Early Growth stages. When Alpha and Beta decline, nothing replaces them. 75% of current revenue is in markets growing ≤ 2% annually.
Takeaway
A balanced portfolio requires products at multiple life-cycle stages — not just profitable products today.
